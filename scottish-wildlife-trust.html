Who are the Scottish Wildlife Trust

The Scottish Wildlife Trust are a membership-based charity what were formed in 1964 with the goal to "advance the conservation of Scotland's biodiversity for the benefit of present and future generations


what have they done

The Scottish Wildlife Trust have done a varity of diffrent things for the enviroment. For example they have been able to get bevers on the list of protected speicies in scotland and won an award for this at the 2019 nature of scotland awards, They have 40,000 members for the trust, 1,000 volunteers and 120 wildlife reserves within the 50+ years they have been in service. more information of what they have achieved at https://scottishwildlifetrust.org.uk They also have a youtube channel which shows you all the diffrent types of things they do from observing diffrent animals, asking experts about the enviroment and other videos designed to help you learn more about why its important what they do. click the link here to view the channel ScottishWildlifeTrustvideos
