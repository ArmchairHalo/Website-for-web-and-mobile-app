Fife college


It's history

on 1/08/2013 fife college was created as a merger of Adam smith college,Carnegie college and non-land elements of the Elmwood Campus of the college SRUC. When this was announced the principal for Motherwell college Hugh Logan would become the new principle for fife college. 3 years after being the principal he would announce his retirement and on 1/03/2017 Hugh Hall was appointed as the new principal for fife college.
